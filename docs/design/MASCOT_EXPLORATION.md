# SavePixie Mascot Exploration

Status: active exploration before identity approval  
Updated: 2026-07-15

## Objective

Choose a Pixie that is simple enough to work as an app icon and tiny in-product reaction, cute enough to create affection, and distinctive enough to become an ownable SavePixie symbol.

## Non-negotiable traits

- one strong silhouette visible at 24–32 px;
- gender-neutral and non-human;
- cute and emotionally readable without looking designed only for children;
- a visual connection to saving through a glowing goal seed or integrated seed pouch;
- wings or a floating light element that communicate “pixie” without a generic fairy costume;
- a face that can express calm, curiosity, focus, success, celebration, recovery, and error with very few moving parts;
- reproducible proportions and materials across every screen and animation;
- original and clearly separated from existing educational, entertainment, banking, and crypto mascots.

## Selection tests

Each candidate must pass all five tests.

1. **Icon test:** recognisable as a silhouette in a small circular app icon.
2. **Emotion test:** readable expressions using eyes, brows, posture, and one or two simple effects.
3. **Motion test:** capable of idle, hover, save, progress, recovery, and celebration animations without excessive detail.
4. **Audience test:** appealing to a teenager, a first-job adult, and a playful older adult.
5. **Product test:** feels like a saving companion, not a fantasy-game character placed on top of a finance app.

## Directions under exploration

### A — Seedling Sprite

A round, soft-bodied companion with a luminous sprout crest, tiny leaf-star wings, and an integrated belly pouch. The cutest and simplest direction, with excellent icon potential.

### B — Petal Hood Pixie

A refined version of the current hooded character: a smaller face, rounder body, shorter hood, clearer seed motif, and fewer fabric details. Warm and expressive while retaining a distinctive pointed silhouette.

### C — Star Moth Pixie

A compact central body framed by two large petal-moth wings, with the glowing goal seed held at its centre. The wings provide the icon silhouette and change subtly with emotional state.

### D — Wishdrop Pixie

A minimal floating droplet/star form with tiny limbs, expressive crescent eyes, and a seed light inside its body. The most abstract and scalable direction, but it must retain warmth and avoid becoming a generic app assistant.

## Approval sequence

1. Compare the four silhouettes at equal scale.
2. Select one direction or one controlled hybrid.
3. Create a turnaround and expression sheet for the winner.
4. Test the winner in a tiny app icon, a Today screen, and a celebration state.
5. Freeze proportions, materials, palette, face construction, wing construction, seed/pouch, and animation rules in the style guide.

No final app screens should be generated until the mascot anchor is approved.
