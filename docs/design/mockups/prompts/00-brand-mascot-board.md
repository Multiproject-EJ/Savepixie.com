# Prompt: SavePixie brand and mascot board

Use case: ui-mockup

Asset type: high-fidelity mobile-app brand and mascot direction board

Primary request: Create the visual identity anchor for SavePixie, a playful but premium savings and simple-budget app for young adults and playful adults. The board must establish one original, gender-neutral savings-pixie mascot and a cohesive modern mobile interface system that later screen mockups can follow.

Subject: Show the same compact pixie character in five clearly consistent expressions or poses: calm idle, curious prompt, focused coaching, small success, and joyful celebration. The pixie has a strong readable silhouette, expressive eyes and brows, luminous star- or leaf-shaped wings, and a small cross-body pouch holding a glowing goal seed. Youthful but not babyish. Magical but not fantasy-costume-heavy.

Style/medium: polished stylised 3D or premium 2.5D character rendering combined with realistic shippable mobile UI components; sleek product-design presentation, not concept-art scenery.

Composition/framing: landscape brand board with a large mascot anchor, the five smaller consistent mascot states, colour swatches, typography hierarchy, tactile buttons, a savings progress card, a circular progress indicator, an XP/Stardust bar, and a simple four-item mobile bottom navigation preview.

Color palette: midnight indigo and deep plum foundation, electric violet brand colour, fresh mint or aqua for positive progress, warm gold for meaningful milestones, soft lavender-white text and surfaces, coral used only as a tiny attention accent.

Lighting/mood: luminous, optimistic, calm, premium and energetic; subtle depth and glow; crisp readable contrast.

Text (verbatim): "SavePixie", "TODAY", "GOALS", "PLAN", "JOURNEY", "6 DAY STREAK", "£420 OF £1,200".

Constraints: one coherent mascot identity across every pose; mobile-first usability; large readable hierarchy; rounded cards with crisp edges and restrained translucent depth; original character and UI; no bank logos; no coins raining from the sky; no gambling or casino imagery; no shame or alarm states; no clutter; no excessive glassmorphism; no watermark.

Avoid: resemblance to Duolingo, Disney, existing game mascots, crypto products, childish classroom apps, corporate banking dashboards, generic fairy costumes, slot-machine rewards, dense analytics charts.
